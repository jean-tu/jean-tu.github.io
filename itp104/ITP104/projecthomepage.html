About

     Thinking about visiting the Bay Area up in Northern California and don't know where you want to go or what to do? Well I have good news for you!! This website will give you the basics of what you need to know before visiting. Explore the North, East, and South bay of the Bay Area.

Fun facts about the Bay Area:

  • The chinese Fortune Cookie originated in San Fransisco. Makoto Hagiwara created the first Fortune Cookie in 1914.
  • Joey Chestnut, the fastest hot-dog eater, is from San Jose, California
  • The largest Monopoly board in the world is in San Jose in Guadalupe River Park
