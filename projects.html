LinkedIn Hackathon: GuardHäus


Summer 2017

On the 14th of July 2017, there were a group of 6 females that gathered together to help make the world a safer place. At a LinkedIn Office in Sunnyvale. Check out the links below to see what we were working on. We were fortunate to have made it past the preliminary round and make it to the finals; even though we may not have placed in the top 3, it was still a wonderful experience being able to work with these bright young women. Our group was called "The House of Guards" and we did that because it was a play off of the House of Cards and the application that we had built was called GuardHäus.
