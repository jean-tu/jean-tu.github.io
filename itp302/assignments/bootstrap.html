Get Involved

ISF is all about collaboration and we encourage you to get involved. Donate, volunteer your time, use your creativity to raise funds for ISF, use your voice to raise awareness…the possibilities are limitless!

CLICK HERE to read one person's perspective about getting involved with the ISF.

Give

Donations to ISF make all the difference to the health and well-being of our planet and all of its creatures. Your donations will help fund projects that not only make an impact today, but have long term impact. There are many ways to provide financial support for ISF’s mission. Discover the donation option that works best for you.

GenISF, College and Community Crews

ISF Crews are independent, self-driven and are formed on their own initiative for the purpose of conducting charitable fundraising, education, and community oriented projects and events in support of ISF’s charitable mission. Join an existing Crew or Form your own.

Volunteer

Are you ready to roll up your sleeves and get involved? Tell us a bit about yourself and what you are passionate about - let’s see if ISF has the right volunteer position for you.

YOUTH and COLLEGE Advisory Boards

Are you passionate about change? Consider becoming an IS Foundation MOBstir or CAB member. Keep checking the Youth or College Page and follow them on Twitter @ISFCollege and @ISFYouth to find out when they are accepting new applications.

SHARE Your Ideas

FUNDRAISE
